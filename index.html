St Peters Community Preschool is a special place in the Inner West of Sydney where children, families, educators and the wider community play, learn and grow together.

Our program provides children with the freedom and support to explore their world through play, learn about relationships, express their creativity, and develop positive attitudes to learning in a safe, loving and stimulating environment.
